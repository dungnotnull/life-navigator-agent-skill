# Help Resources — Free & Low-Cost by Document Type

## 🏥 Medical & Health Resources

### Medical Bills & Insurance
- **Patient Advocate Foundation** — Free case managers for people with serious illness
  Phone: 1-800-532-5274 | patientadvocate.org
- **Hospital Financial Assistance / Charity Care** — Ask the hospital's billing department directly
  Say: "Do you have a financial assistance or charity care program?"
- **NeedyMeds** — Prescription assistance programs, free clinics
  Phone: 1-800-503-6897 | needymeds.org
- **Partnership for Prescription Assistance** — pparx.org
- **RxAssist** — rxassist.org — pharmaceutical company patient assistance programs
- **GoodRx** — goodrx.com — compare drug prices and coupons

### Medicare Specific
- **SHIP (State Health Insurance Assistance Program)** — FREE, unbiased Medicare counseling
  Find your state: shiphelp.org | Call Medicare: 1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227)
- **Medicare Rights Center** — medicarerights.org | 1-800-333-4114
- **BenefitsCheckUp** — benefitscheckup.org — find programs for seniors
- **Extra Help / LIS** — Low-income drug cost help: ssa.gov/extrahelp or call SSA: 1-800-772-1213

### Medicaid
- **Medicaid.gov** — official info
- **Benefits.gov** — benefits.gov — find programs you may qualify for
- **Legal Aid** in your state — can help with Medicaid denials and appeals

### Insurance Appeals
- **State Insurance Commissioner** — Every state has one; handles complaints against insurers
  Find yours: naic.org/state_web_map.htm
- **External Review** — Right to independent review of denied claims (free)
  Ask your insurer for an External Review Request form

## 🏠 Housing Resources

### Eviction / Housing Crisis
- **211** — Dial 2-1-1 or visit 211.org — local emergency housing resources
- **National Low Income Housing Coalition** — nlihc.org
- **Legal Aid in your area** — Free legal help for eviction cases
  Find: lawhelp.org or lsc.gov/find-legal-aid
- **HUD-approved Housing Counselors** — Free! hud.gov/find-a-housing-counselor
  Or call: 1-800-569-4287
- **Emergency Rental Assistance Programs** — Search "[your state] emergency rental assistance"
- **NLIHC Emergency Rental Assistance Finder** — nlihc.org/era

### Utilities
- **LIHEAP (Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program)** — help paying heating/cooling bills
  Find: liheap.acf.hhs.gov or call 211
- **Utility company's own assistance programs** — call the utility and ask about:
  "Do you have payment plans or low-income assistance programs?"
- **Project Share / Warm Neighbor programs** — local utility nonprofit funds

### Homeownership / Mortgage
- **HUD Foreclosure Prevention Counselors** — Free! 1-800-569-4287
- **Making Home Affordable / CFPB** — consumerfinance.gov
- **State-specific Hardest Hit Fund** — check your state housing finance agency

### Section 8 / Public Housing
- **Local Public Housing Authority (PHA)** — google "[your city] Housing Authority"
- **HUD** — hud.gov | 1-800-955-2232

## 💰 Financial & Debt Resources

### Debt & Collections
- **National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC)** — nfcc.org | 1-800-388-2227
  Free and low-cost credit counseling
- **Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)** — consumerfinance.gov
  Can file complaints about debt collectors
- **Federal Trade Commission** — ftc.gov — file debt collector complaints
- **Debt Validation Rights** — You have 30 days to dispute a debt in writing
  Address disputes to the collection agency in writing (certified mail)

### IRS / Tax Issues
- **Taxpayer Advocate Service (TAS)** — Independent IRS help. FREE.
  IRS.gov/advocate | 1-877-777-4778
- **Free Tax Preparation (VITA)** — IRS.gov/VITA | 1-800-906-9887
- **Low Income Taxpayer Clinics (LITC)** — taxpayeradvocate.irs.gov/litc
  Free legal representation against IRS for low-income taxpayers
- **IRS Payment Plans (Online)** — irs.gov/payments — set up installment agreement
- **IRS Offer in Compromise Pre-Qualifier** — irs.gov/OIC

### Student Loans
- **Federal Student Aid** — studentaid.gov | 1-800-433-3243
- **Student Loan Borrower Assistance** — studentloanborrowerassistance.org
- **National Student Legal Defense Network** — nsldn.org
- **Income-Driven Repayment (IDR) Application** — studentaid.gov/idr

### Bankruptcy
- **Legal Aid** — may provide free bankruptcy consultations
- **American Bankruptcy Institute** — abiworld.org — find local attorneys
- **Many bankruptcy attorneys offer free initial consultations**

## 🏛️ Government & Legal Resources

### Social Security
- **SSA Office** — Call 1-800-772-1213 | ssa.gov
- **Medicare Rights Center** — 1-800-333-4114
- **National Organization of Social Security Claimants' Representatives (NOSSCR)** — Find SS attorneys: nosscr.org
- **Disability Rights Advocates** — dralegal.org

### Legal Help (General)
- **Legal Aid Society** — legalaid.org (nationwide)
- **LawHelp.org** — lawhelp.org — find free legal help in your state
- **LSC (Legal Services Corporation)** — lsc.gov/find-legal-aid
- **American Bar Association Free Legal Help** — americanbar.org/groups/legal_aid_indigent_defendants
- **Pro Bono Coordinators** — most state bar associations have pro bono programs
- **Law School Clinics** — many law schools offer free clinics for common issues

### Immigration
- **USCIS** — uscis.gov | 1-800-375-5283
- **National Immigration Law Center** — nilc.org
- **Immigration Advocates Network** — immigrationadvocates.org — find free immigration legal help
- **Accredited Representatives** — immigration help for low-income: DOJ list at justice.gov/eoir
- **WARNING: Notarios / Document Preparers** — Cannot give legal advice. Use only DOJ-accredited representatives or licensed attorneys for complex immigration matters.

### Courts & Legal Matters
- **Court Self-Help Centers** — most courthouses have one; free guidance on paperwork
- **Lawline / AVVO** — online legal questions answered by attorneys
- **Your State's Lawyer Referral Service** — first consultations often $25-$50
