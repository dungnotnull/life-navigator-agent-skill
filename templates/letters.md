# Letter Templates — When You Need to Write Back

## How to Use

These are fill-in-the-blank letters for common response situations.
Replace everything in [BRACKETS] with your actual information.
Print or email as appropriate.

---

## LETTER-01: Debt Validation Request (to Collection Agency)

**Send by:** Certified Mail with Return Receipt
**When:** Within 30 days of receiving a collection letter

---

[YOUR FULL NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[CITY, STATE ZIP]
[DATE]

[COLLECTION AGENCY NAME]
[COLLECTION AGENCY ADDRESS]

**Re: Debt Validation Request — Account/Reference Number: [NUMBER FROM THEIR LETTER]**

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing in response to your letter dated [DATE OF THEIR LETTER].
I am requesting that you provide verification of this debt as required
under the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), 15 U.S.C. § 1692g.

Please provide the following:
1. The name and address of the original creditor
2. The amount of the debt, including a breakdown of principal, interest, and fees
3. Proof that your company has the legal right to collect this debt
4. A copy of any written agreement creating this debt

Until this debt is verified, please cease all collection activities.

I am not acknowledging that this debt is valid or that I owe any amount.

Sincerely,

[YOUR SIGNATURE]
[YOUR PRINTED NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[YOUR PHONE — optional]

## LETTER-02: Insurance Appeal Letter

**Send by:** Certified Mail with Return Receipt
**When:** Before the appeal deadline in your denial letter

---

[YOUR FULL NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[DATE]

[INSURANCE COMPANY NAME]
Appeals Department
[ADDRESS FROM YOUR DENIAL LETTER]

**Re: Appeal of Claim Denial — Member ID: [YOUR ID] — Claim #: [CLAIM NUMBER]**

Dear Appeals Department:

I am writing to formally appeal the denial of my claim dated [DATE OF DENIAL]
for [DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE] provided by [PROVIDER NAME] on [DATE OF SERVICE].

**Reason for Denial (as stated in your letter):** [COPY THE DENIAL REASON]

**Why This Service Is Necessary:**
[In 2-3 sentences, explain why you needed this service. Example: "My doctor,
Dr. [Name], ordered this [test/treatment] because [brief medical reason].
Without it, I risk [brief consequence]."]

**Supporting Documentation Enclosed:**
- Letter of Medical Necessity from Dr. [Doctor's Name] (attached)
- [Any other relevant documents]

I request that you reconsider this denial and approve coverage for this service.

If this appeal is denied, please provide information about my right to an
External Review and the process for requesting one.

Sincerely,

[YOUR SIGNATURE]
[YOUR PRINTED NAME]
[YOUR MEMBER ID]
[PHONE NUMBER]

because [choose and briefly explain]:

    significant financial hardship to my business.

[ ] I have a pre-scheduled medical procedure/treatment on [date] that cannot be rescheduled.

[ ] Other: [Brief explanation]

Respectfully,

[YOUR SIGNATURE]
