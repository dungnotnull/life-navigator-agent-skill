# Life Navigator Agent — Quick Reference

## Urgency Levels
| Level | Symbol | When | Response Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| URGENT | 🔴 | Eviction, shutoff, IRS levy, court summons, immigration NTA | TODAY |
| TIME-SENSITIVE | 🟡 | Insurance denial, SS overpayment, COBRA | 1–2 weeks |
| NORMAL | 🟢 | Routine bills, statements, standard notices | As directed |
| INFORMATIONAL | ℹ️ | EOBs, annual summaries, informational letters | No action needed |

## Special Modes
| Mode | Triggers | Key Adjustments |
|---|---|---|
| 👴 Senior | Medicare, SS, age 65+, parent | Grade 5-6 level, SHIP/SMP resources, scam extra warning |
| 🌍 Immigrant | USCIS, visa, green card, "my English" | Ultra-short sentences, DOJ rep recommendation |
| 💊 Caregiver | "my mother/father", managing for | HIPAA note, POA consideration, caregiver resources |
| 🚨 Crisis | Eviction, shutoff, levy, NTA, CPS | Emergency resources FIRST, emotional acknowledgment |

## Top 10 Scam Red Flags
1. Payment by gift cards demanded
2. Wire transfer or cryptocurrency demanded
3. Threatening immediate arrest
4. No specific account number or name
5. Poor spelling/grammar throughout
6. Requests your full SSN by phone/email
7. Medicare calling to "update your card"
8. SSA says your number is "suspended"
9. IRS says arrest warrant issued by phone
10. "Don't tell your family" instruction
